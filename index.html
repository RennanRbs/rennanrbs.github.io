GloboPlay

Globoplay is an online streaming platform operated by Globo, a major media network in Brazil. It offers a wide range of content, including TV shows, series, news, and reality shows like "Big Brother Brasil" (BBB). The website features categories such as "Now on TV," "Novels," "Series," "Journalism," and a comprehensive catalog. This platform is primarily designed for viewers interested in Globo's diverse programming, providing access to current and past shows, live broadcasts, and exclusive content. It's a significant destination for fans of Brazilian television and entertainment.

Yellow C6 Bank

The C6 Yellow is a digital banking account for minors under 18 years, offered by C6 Bank. It provides features like a debit card, the ability to set savings goals, and parental monitoring options. The account is designed to help young people learn financial management in a practical and interactive way. Parents or guardians can oversee the account's activities, ensuring safety and security in its usage. This initiative facilitates financial education for the youth while offering a real-world banking experience. For more details, you can visit their website.

ITI Itau bank

The iti app by Itaú is a digital banking platform that offers a variety of banking services. Users can open a free digital account quickly, enjoy a credit card without annual fees, and manage financial goals with 100% CDI earnings. The app provides security features of Itaú, transparent fee structures, and recycled material cards with braille. It's designed for convenient, secure, and inclusive financial management, accessible via both the Apple Store and Google Play. For more detailed information, you can visit their website.

Lonjong App

The Lojong app is a meditation and mind training platform. It offers guided meditations, introductory videos on meditation concepts, and a customized timer for silent meditations. The app is designed to help users reduce stress, anxiety, and improve sleep quality. It also provides educational content on the scientific benefits of meditation, including its effects on mental and physical health. Lojong includes features like daily mindfulness reminders, anxiety management tools, and inspirational quotes. It's available for iOS and Android users.
